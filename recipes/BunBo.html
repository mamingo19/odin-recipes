Bun Bo`

Description

Bun Bo is a Vietnamese noodle dish renowned for its aromatic broth and tender beef slices. This flavorful concoction combines rich beef broth with a variety of spices, served over rice vermicelli noodles and topped with fresh herbs, crunchy bean sprouts, and zesty lime wedges. The dish is further enhanced by the addition of fish sauce and sriracha, offering a symphony of flavors that tantalize the taste buds.

Ingredients:

For the broth:

  • 4-5 lbs beef bones
  • 1 large onion, halved
  • 1 piece of ginger (about 4 inches), sliced
  • Lemongrass, dried chili peppers, cinnamon stick, star anise, coriander seeds
  • Salt and sugar
  • Water

For the dish:

  • 1 lb beef sirloin, thinly sliced
  • Rice vermicelli noodles
  • Onion, green onions, bean sprouts, cilantro, Thai basil
  • Lime wedges
  • Fish sauce
  • Sriracha sauce

Steps:

  1. Prepare Broth:
    • Roast beef bones.
    • Combine bones, onion, ginger, spices, salt, and sugar in a stockpot.
    • Cover with water, boil, then simmer for 4-6 hours.
  2. Prepare Toppings:
    • Slice onion, chop green onions, cilantro, and Thai basil.
    • Prepare lime wedges and bean sprouts.
  3. Cook Noodles:
    • Cook rice vermicelli noodles.
  4. Assemble Bun Bo:
    • Strain broth.
    • Arrange noodles in bowls, top with sliced beef.
  5. Serve:
    • Pour hot broth over noodles and beef.
    • Serve with prepared toppings, fish sauce, and sriracha.
